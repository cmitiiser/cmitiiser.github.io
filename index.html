Welcome to CMIT!

The IISER Thiruvananthapuram Math Club

‘Club of Mathematics, IISER Thiruvananthapuram’ or CMIT, as we call it, is a student-run, department recognized, open Mathematics club at the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research, Thiruvananthapuram — with objectives of promoting Mathematics and provide an environment for the student community to discuss, learn and indulge in various activities throughout the year. Learn more about the club on our about and activities page, or read our blog and newsletter.
