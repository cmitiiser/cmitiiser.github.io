About INTER IISER-NISER MATHEMATICS MEET

[IINMM - 2024]

The Inter-IISER-NISER Mathematics Meet is a joint initiative of all the IISERs and NISER. The aim of this meet is to provide a common platform to exchange ideas and promote fruitful collaborative research across these institutes. It also provides an opportunity to the PhD students and postdoctoral fellow to showcase their research works and interact with other researchers in related fields. The previous IINMMs were hosted by IISER Pune (2017), IISER Bhopal (2018), IISER Mohali (2020), IISER Tirupati (2021), IISER Kolkata (2022) and NISER Bhubaneswar (2023).

About IISER Thiruvananthapuram

Indian Institute of Science Education and Research Thiruvananthapuram (IISER Thiruvananthapuram) is one of the premier science education and research institutes established by the Government of India in 2008 to impart high-quality education integrated with outstanding research in modern science. Within the last fourteen years since its inception, it has risen to establish itself as one of the premier institutes in our country.


How to Reach IISER Thiruvananthapuram

IISER Thiruvananthapuram campus is located in Vithura village, in the north-eastern part of Thiruvananthapuram city.

The campus is about 7 Km from Vithura Junction. Vithura is a small village situated at about 32 km from Thiruvananthapuram. Vithura serves as a pit-stop on the way to the beautiful Ponmudi hill station.

From the Airport:

IISER Thiruvananthapuram is about 45 km from the airport. A taxi will cost around Rs. 1800/- from Airport to IISER Thiruvananthapuram permanent campus.

From the Railway Station/KSRTC Central bus station:

IISER Thiruvananthapuram is about 40 km from Thiruvananthapuram Central railway station/KSRTC central bus station popularly known as Thampanoor. KSRTC (Kerala State Road Transport Corporation) bus services are available from KSRTC Central bus station (Situated opposite to Thiruvananthapuram Central Railway station) to IISER Thiruvananthapuram, Vithura campus.
