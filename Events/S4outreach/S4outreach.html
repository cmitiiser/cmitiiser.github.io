CMIT S4 Outreach Program for Schools

Club of Mathematics, IISER Thiruvananthapuram conducted an Outreach Program


The outreach program was be held on 18th September 2022 (Sunday). The aim of this program was to inclulcate interest towards Mathematics in School Kids and encourage Mathematical Creativity.
Please find the schedule for the outreach Program below!

Schedule

Sunday, September 18, 2022


Event Time Venue
Innaugration 09:30AM to 09:45AM Bhaskara, Lecture Hall Complex
Rangoli : Connecting Dots Competition 10:10AM to 11:45AM Corridor, Lecture Hall Complex
Presentation: Chalk & Talk 10:15AM to 12:15PM Bhaskara, Lecture Hall Complex
GanitWiz : Quiz (Prelims) 01:15PM to 02:00PM Jupiter, Lecture Hall Complex
Faculty Talk: Are all triangles equilateral? (Dr Shrihari Sridharan) 02:00PM to 02:30PM Bhaskara, Lecture Hall Complex
Student talk: The Königsberg bridges: Pathway to Graph theory (Anitha Valliapan, Int. Ph.D.) 02:30PM to 03:00PM Bhaskara, Lecture Hall Complex
Quiz : GanitWiz Finals 03:00PM to 04:30PM Bhaskara, Lecture Hall Complex
Prize Distribution 04:30PM to 05:00PM Bhasakara, Lecture Hall Complex
