CMIT S4 Outreach Program for Schools

Club of Mathematics IISER TVM is conducting CMIT OP a Fun-Math outreach program for students in classes 8th to 12th. The main aim of the program is to let the younger generation explore the world of mathematics.

The outreach program will be held on 18th September 2022 (Sunday). This program is planned to be held from 9:30 AM to 5:00 PM, with refreshments and lunch provided by the committee within the campus. The college provides a pickup bus from Thampanoor Bus Stop to IISER TVM at 7:15 AM and leaves for Thampanoor Bus Stop at 5:00 PM. Each student can be accompanied by a maximum of 2 guardians. Students are supposed to fill out the Google form provided below. Following is the list of events being held for the day:

Sponsors: School of Mathematics IISER Thiruvananthapuram, and, Science and Technology Council IISER Thiruvananthapuram.

Registration and participation in the event are totally free of cost, as our main aim is to let students experience the IISER TVM campus and get a good exposition to Mathematics. Exciting prizes await the winners and participation certificates will be given to all participants.
